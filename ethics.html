Applications of Facial Recognition Technology

Facial Recognition Technology (FRT) is being used in a variety of fields to improve convenience, enhance security, and deliver personalized experiences.

Security & Surveillance

Used by law enforcement to identify suspects, track movements, and enhance airport security systems.

Smartphones & Devices

Popular for unlocking phones, verifying identities for apps, and processing mobile payments like Face ID.

Retail & Marketing

Retailers use FRT to analyze shopping behavior, prevent theft, and create tailored customer experiences.

Healthcare

FRT helps with patient check-ins, access control, and identity verification in hospitals and clinics.

Social Media & Entertainment

Platforms use it for automatic photo tagging, filters, and creating personalized avatars or experiences.

Banking & Finance

Banks use it for fraud prevention, customer authentication, and ATM access without cards.
