Ethical Frameworks in FRT Analysis:

We analyzed FRT using three ethical frameworks:

  • Deontological Ethics: Focuses on duties and privacy rights.
  • Utilitarianism: Weighs public safety benefits against potential individual harm.
  • Virtue Ethics: Considers responsible use and fairness.

Why These Lenses Matter:
